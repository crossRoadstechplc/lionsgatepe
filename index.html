About

A platform for infrastructure capital

Lionsgate is a private equity platform focused on infrastructure investment across Africa. The firm partners with institutional, sovereign, and strategic investors to develop critical assets across energy, transport, and logistics.

We originate and structure transactions where institutional discipline meets long-horizon development — aligning capital with assets that strengthen trade corridors, power grids, and urban mobility for generations.

Investment focus

Where we deploy capital

01

Generation, transmission, and distributed assets that support Africa's energy transition and grid resilience.

02

03

Cross-border connectivity

Regional highways, bridges, and digital-physical corridors that integrate markets across borders.

Approach

How we work with partners

  1. Originate

    We identify platform and project opportunities alongside governments, SOEs, and strategic corporates — prioritizing assets with clear regulatory pathways and contracted cash flows.

  2. Structure

    Transactions are built for institutional co-investment: robust governance, transparent reporting, and risk frameworks aligned with long-term infrastructure holders.

  3. Partner

    We remain engaged through construction and operations — supporting management teams and sponsors to deliver on development milestones and community outcomes.
